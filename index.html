About The RGV Notary

Professional & prompt

Texas commissioned notary public

The RGV Notary provides convenient mobile notary services across Cameron, Hidalgo & Willacy Counties and the wider Rio Grande Valley.

Local, flexible mobile notary for real life paperwork

We help individuals, families and small businesses get important documents signed and notarized correctly without losing hours out of the day sitting in an office.

Serving clients across Cameron, Hidalgo and Willacy Counties, we travel to homes, hospitals, offices, coffee shops and more. You choose the time and place, we bring the stamp, seal and required ID checks.

Titles, powers of attorney, affidavits and other documents can feel stressful or time sensitive. Our goal is to keep the process simple: clear communication up front, confirmation of what you need ready, and a smooth signing when we meet.
